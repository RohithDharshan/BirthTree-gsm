# BirthTree

## What it is
A private family app that keeps every birthday and anniversary in one shared
yearly calendar and maps the family itself as an interactive tree. Families
share one invite code; members add dates with photos, and reminders go out
automatically (browser, Telegram, ntfy push, daily email digest).

## Who it's for
Families, mostly multi-generational Indian households in practice: one person
sets it up, parents/siblings/cousins join with the family code. Used a few
times a month on phones and laptops, often in the evening.

## Register
Brand for the public landing page (design IS the pitch); product for the
signed-in calendar, tree, and admin surfaces.

## Voice
Warm, personal, unhurried. Celebrations and memory, not productivity.
Plain sentences. No SaaS language.

## Design system (committed)
- Warm premium dark: charcoal `#0e0c09`, champagne gold `#e6b34f` primary
  accent, muted rose `#c96f85` for anniversaries/love, warm off-white text.
- Fraunces (serif, display) + Outfit (sans, body).
- Glass panels, film grain overlay, drifting warm ambient light.
- Motion: framer-motion springs, 3D perspective entrances on scroll.

## Surfaces
- `/welcome` public landing (pre-auth)
- `/` calendar (12-month grid), `/tree` family tree, `/admin` super-admin
- Auth screen (username + password), family setup (create/join)
